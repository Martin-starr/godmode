OUR CRAFT

We roast less, so we can roast better.

NORD is a small-batch roastery in Oslo. We buy fewer coffees, from growers we know, and roast them fresh each week. This is what that means.

HOW WE SOURCE

Direct relationships, not commodity lots.

We buy from importers and farms we have a relationship with, and we pay above commodity price for quality and care. We would rather offer six coffees we can stand behind than sixty we cannot.

HOW WE ROAST

Small batches, every week.

We roast to order in small batches, so the coffee that reaches you is days old, not months. Each profile is built to bring out what the origin already has — not to cover it.

Good coffee is mostly restraint. Source well, roast carefully, then get out of the way.

WHAT'S IN THE BAG

Everything you should know.

Every bag lists the origin, region, process, altitude, and roast date. No anonymous blends, no "100% Arabica" and nothing else. Transparency is the least a roaster owes you.
