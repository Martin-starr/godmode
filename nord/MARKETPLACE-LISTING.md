## How submission works (overview)

1. **Build the template in Framer** (see `FRAMER-BUILD-GUIDE.md`). It must be a
   native Framer project — code can't be uploaded.
2. Go to **framer.com/marketplace** and sign up as a **Creator** (free).
3. In your template project, open the **Marketplace / Submit** flow. Fill in the
   fields below, set the price, and upload preview images.
4. **Framer reviews** it (a few days) against their quality guidelines; they may
   request changes. Fix and resubmit.
5. On approval it goes **live**. Framer processes sales and pays your share via
   their payout system.

## Preview / cover images (what Framer asks for)

Framer shows a **cover/thumbnail** on the marketplace card plus a gallery of page
screenshots. Recommendations:
- **Cover (card):** the hero — single bag on concrete (`assets/img/hero-bag-concrete.jpg`)
  or a clean composition with the NORD wordmark. 16:9, lots of negative space.
- **Gallery:** full-page screenshots of Home, Shop, Product, Our Craft, Wholesale,
  Journal, Contact. Capture from the published Framer site at a desktop width.
- Keep every preview in the same austere register — consistency is the whole effect.

> I can generate a dedicated, polished marketplace **cover image** (with or without
